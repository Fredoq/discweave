# Code of Conduct

This project follows the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a productive community:

- using welcoming and inclusive language;
- respecting differing viewpoints and experiences;
- accepting constructive feedback;
- focusing on what is best for the project;
- showing empathy toward other community members.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, intimidation, or discrimination;
- personal attacks or insulting comments;
- publishing private information without permission;
- sustained disruption of project discussions.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior. Maintainers may remove comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, or other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported through the security contact listed in `SECURITY.md`.
