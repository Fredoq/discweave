# Code of Conduct

DiscWeave uses the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct, version 2.1, as the baseline expectation for project participation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- being respectful of different experience levels and music collecting practices;
- giving constructive, specific feedback;
- keeping discussions focused on the archive product scope;
- respecting privacy around personal collections and local data.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discriminatory language, personal attacks, publishing private information, or pressuring maintainers for unrelated product directions.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when behavior violates these expectations.
